Gossip Girl


About Gossip Girl:

Gossip Girl is a teen drama centered on the scandalous lives of privileged Manhattan prep school teenagers on the Upper East Side. Narrated by an anonymous blogger, the series follows socialites Serena van der Woodsen and Blair Waldorf as they navigate intense friendships, love triangles, and betrayal, all while their secrets are exposed online.

FUN FACT!

Blake Lively (Serena) was the first choice for her role, while Leighton Meester dyed her hair brown to win the part of Blair!
